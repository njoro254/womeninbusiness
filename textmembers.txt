This is an account that is designed for formal and informal women groups of at least 3 members who would want their savings to be safe and at the same time grow.

Features
- Minimum opening and operating balance of Kshs 2000
- Minimum operating balance Kshs 2000
- Interest paid every 6 months
- Savers can choose to receive 6, 12, 18 or 24 months investment scheme.

Benefits
- Zero opening balance
- Attractive interest rates
- Flexible savings period
- Access to loan facilities

Requirements
- Copy of National ID
- Passport photo
- A group of at least 3 members.
